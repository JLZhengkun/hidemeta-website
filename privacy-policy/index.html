HideMeta Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to HideMeta! We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Our Commitment

HideMeta is a privacy-focused application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data whatsoever.

How We Handle Data

1. Local Processing

  • ✓ All photo and metadata processing is done entirely locally on your device
  • ✓ The app does not require internet connection to function
  • ✓ Your photos never leave your device

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We DO NOT collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Photo content or metadata
  • Device information
  • Location information
  • Usage statistics
  • Analytics data
  • Crash reports

3. Photo Access Permission

  • HideMeta requires access to your photo library solely to read and process photos you select
  • All processing is done locally
  • Processed photos are saved on your device
  • We cannot access any of your photos

4. Third-Party Services

  • ✗ No third-party analytics services
  • ✗ No advertising services
  • ✗ No tracking services
  • ✗ No data sharing with any third parties

Data Security

Since all data processing occurs locally on your device, your data security is entirely protected by your device's operating system (iOS/iPadOS/macOS). We have no access to any of your data.

Children's Privacy

HideMeta does not knowingly collect personal information from users of any age, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If our privacy policy changes, we will update this page. Since we don't collect any data, future changes are unlikely to affect your privacy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:

  • App Store Support: Contact the developer through the app's page on the App Store

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data:

  • ✓ There is no data to delete
  • ✓ There is no data to export
  • ✓ There is no data to correct
  • ✓ Your privacy is fully protected
Summary: HideMeta is a truly privacy-respecting application. Our design philosophy is "zero data collection," allowing you to use the app with complete peace of mind.
