Welcome to the official support page for HideMeta. Here you'll find everything you need to know about using the app.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Select Photos

    Tap the "Select Photos" button to choose images from your library. You can select single or multiple photos at once.

  2. Automatic Analysis

    HideMeta instantly scans for sensitive metadata including GPS location, camera model, lens information, and timestamps.

  3. Clean & Save

    Tap the save button. The app will remove all detected metadata and save a clean copy to your library (or overwrite the original depending on your settings).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is my data sent to any server?

A: No. HideMeta works 100% locally on your device. No photos or data are ever uploaded to the internet.

Q: Does it delete my original photos?

A: By default, HideMeta saves a new copy of the photo without metadata. If you use the "Overwrite" feature, the original photo will be modified (after your confirmation).

Q: Why do I need to grant Photo Library access?

A: Accessibility is required to read the photos you select for processing and to save the cleaned versions back to your library.

使用教程

  1. 选择照片

    点击“选择照片”按钮从您的相册中选取图片。支持单张或多张照片批量选择。

  2. 智能分析

    HideMeta 会即时扫描照片中的敏感元数据，包括 GPS 位置信息、相机型号、镜头参数和拍摄时间等。

  3. 清除并保存

    点击保存按钮。应用将移除所有检测到的元数据，并保存一份干净的副本到您的相册（如果您开启了覆盖模式，则会修改原图）。

常见问题 (FAQ)

问：我的数据会上传到服务器吗？

答：不会。HideMeta 100% 在您的设备本地运行。没有任何照片或数据会被上传到互联网。

问：会删除我的原始照片吗？

答：默认情况下，HideMeta 会保存一份去除元数据的新副本。如果您使用了“覆盖原图”功能，在您确认后会直接修改原始照片。

问：为什么需要相册访问权限？

答：我们需要访问权限来读取您选定要处理的照片，并将处理后的安全照片保存回您的相册。
